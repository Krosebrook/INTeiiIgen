# Getting Started with DashGen

This tutorial walks you through creating your first data-driven dashboard in DashGen.

**Time to complete:** 10 minutes

## Prerequisites
- A Replit account (for authentication)
- A CSV, JSON, or Excel file with data (or use cloud storage)

---

## Step 1: Login

1. Visit the DashGen application
2. Click **"Login with Replit"** on the landing page
3. Authorize DashGen to access your Replit profile

---

## Step 2: Upload Your Data

1. Navigate to **Upload** from the sidebar
2. Drag and drop a file (CSV, JSON, or Excel) into the upload zone
3. Wait for the upload to complete (progress bar shows status)
4. Your file appears in **Data Sources**

**Tip:** For large files, use the Cloud connectors (Google Drive, OneDrive) instead.

---

## Step 3: Create a Dashboard

1. Navigate to **Dashboards** from the sidebar
2. Click **"Create Dashboard"**
3. Enter a name (e.g., "Sales Analysis")
4. Click **Create**

---

## Step 4: Add Widgets

1. Open your new dashboard
2. Click **"Add Widget"**
3. Choose a widget type:
   - **Bar Chart**: Compare categories
   - **Line Chart**: Show trends over time
   - **Pie Chart**: Show proportions
   - **Table**: Display raw data
   - **Metric**: Single KPI value

4. Select your data source
5. Choose columns for X and Y axes
6. Click **Add**

---

## Step 5: Get AI Insights

1. Go to **Insights** from the sidebar
2. Select a data source
3. Click **"Analyze"**
4. AI generates patterns, anomalies, and recommendations

---

## Step 6: Share Your Dashboard

1. Open your dashboard
2. Click **Share** in the toolbar
3. Toggle **Public** to enable sharing
4. Copy the share link
5. Anyone with the link can view (read-only)
